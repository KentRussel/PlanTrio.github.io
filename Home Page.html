Featured Products

Bonsai
It is the Japanese version of the traditional Chinese art called "penjing". Bonsai is known for its very distinctive design that mimics the big trees that is put into a small pot.
Red Aglaonema
Aglaonema is a flowering plant genus in the Araceae family of arums. They are usually seen in the tropics and subtropics. These plants are also available in a variety of colors
Flase Aralia (Plerandra Elegantissima)
False aralia or the so called "Plerandra elegantissima" is a popular houseplant with wonderfully textured leaves and deeply serrated leaflets that start off copper or burgundy and later deepen to a rich green. It has slender, coppery red to dark green leaves with serrated margins and 7-11 leaflets.
Elephant Ears (Alocasia Odora)
Alocasia Odora or the Elephant ears are tropical perennial plants valued for the huge leaves rather than the blooms. The common name for many species in three plant genera is elephant ear.
Inchplant (Tradescantia Zebrina)
Tradescantia zebrina features zebra-patterned leaves with purple new growth and green older growth parallel to the central axis, as well as two broad silver-colored stripes on the outer borders and a deep uniform magenta on the bottom leaf surface.
Aglaonema
A different variation of the Red one, but still has the same characteristics just in different color.
